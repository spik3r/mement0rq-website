about

I'm a few months into bug bounty hunting on HackerOne & Bugcrowd. Background in software engineering; pivoted into offensive security after one too many vulnerable APIs landed on my doorstep at work.

Currently focused on attack-surface discovery and pre-auth web bugs. I write up everything I learn — the blog is bootstrapping.
